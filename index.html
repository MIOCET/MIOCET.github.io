What is MIOCET?

MIOCET™, otherwise known as the Minecraft Institute Of Consumer Engineering Technology™, is a University located in the United States of Minecraft, in the city of Mineland.

MIOCET™, as the name implies, is an Engineering School, where we teach you all you will need to know to be a sucessful engineer. We offer roughly 10 degrees, including (but not limited to)...

  1. Redstone Programming
  2. Weapons Engineering
  3. Structural Engineering
  4. Gravitational Physics

Armed with a degree from MIOCET™, you can then go on to be a part of our graduate school, where we offer Master's and Doctorate degrees. With MIOCET™ being the top engineering school in the United States of Minecraft, a degree earned here is your golden ticket to success in whichever field you choose.



Our Mission

Our mission here at the Minecraft Institute Of Consumer Engineering Technology™ is to provide students with the skills they need to succeed in this digital world of the United States of Minecraft.
